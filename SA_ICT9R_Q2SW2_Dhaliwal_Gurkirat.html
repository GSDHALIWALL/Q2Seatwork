The Different breed of Dogs

Golden Retriever The Golden Retriever is a Scottish breed and is a medium size dog.
Boston Terrier The Boston Terrier is from the USA, and has an average life span of 13-15 years.
Labrador Retriever The Labrador Retriever is a British breed, A medium sized dog and has an average life span of 10-12 years
Poodle The Poodle is a German breed and has an average life span of 12-15 years
Border Collie The Border Collie is a British breed, it is a very intelligent dog and has a lifespan of 10-17 years
Beagle The Beagle is a British Breed, and has a lifespan of 12-15 years
Irish Setter The Irish Setter is an Irish Breed, and has a lifespan of 12-15 years
Shih Tzu The Shih Tzu is a Tibet breed, and has a lifespan of 10-16 years
Bulldog The Bulldog is a British Breed, and has a lifespan of only 8-10 years
Siberian Husky The Siberian Husky is a Siberian Breed, and has a lifespan of 12-15 years
